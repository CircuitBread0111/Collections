The Visual Organizer for Collectors

Vision Statement

For collectors who need an easy and convenient way to organize their collection, Collections is a visual organizer that provides a simple and visual way to organize collections. Unlike spreadsheets, which are wordy and complicated, our product solves this issue by visually organizing your items to your liking.

What is Collections?

Collections is a convenient way to organize all of your collections. Be it coins, cards, stamps, or games, Collections provides a visual organizer that makes the task of cataloging your collections a breeze.

Planned Features

  • Create and name your collection
  • Add and label items
  • Attach Descriptions to items or collections
  • Attach images to items or collections
