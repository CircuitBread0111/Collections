Audiobooks

Emily has recently been listening to audiobooks and found how useful they are for students who struggle to read long novels or text. She thought it would be a great idea to read some books out loud and record her voice.

After recording several novels, she saw that she had a lot of audio files and wanted to organize them with the books she had physical versions of. Emily thought that Collections would be a great way to add a link to the audio so that anyone who looks at her collection could listen to it as well.
