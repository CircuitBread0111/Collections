Game Store

James has fall break and has decided to return home for a couple of days. Back home, there is a store that he likes to get games from: Bob’s Used Game Store. Every time he returns home, he makes a trip to Bob’s store to check if they have anything he has been looking for, since he has pretty good luck there.

James uses Collections to keep a list of games he is actively looking for. When he enters the store, he goes up to the counter and asks the cashier to look up the inventory status of a couple games. Upon seeing that a couple games are in stock, he buys them, and marks them as obtained in Collections. The entries are moved out of his wishlist, and into his collection.

Later, after he returns home, he goes onto the computer to add details about his specific copy of each of the games.
