Jordan, 26


What is your current job or occupation?

Assembly Production at General Electric

Do you collect anything as a hobby?

Yes, I collect retro video games.

What would you like to see from this kind of product?

I would like to organize my items by value.

What is your technical background?

Above average, I use the computer every day.

What is your current organizational system for your collections?

I organize my collection by game console.

How large is your collection?

Around 70 units.

Is there any part of your collection you need to keep track of?

I want to keep track of the number of hours I have spent playing games.
